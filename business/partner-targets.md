# Partner Targets
## Introduction
The crypto-shield product aims to provide a comprehensive security and custody management solution for businesses handling crypto payments. To enhance its value proposition and expand its reach, we have identified key partner targets for integration. The following section outlines a partner integration roadmap, highlighting 7 specific SaaS/APIs to integrate with, along with their rationale, free-tier limits, integration effort, and value-add.

## Partner Integration Roadmap

1. **Coinbase API**
	* Rationale: Coinbase is a leading cryptocurrency exchange and payment platform, making it an ideal partner for crypto-shield.
	* Free-tier limits: 100 requests per day
	* Integration effort: Medium (M)
	* Value-add: Simplifies crypto payment processing for businesses, reducing the risk of fraudulent transactions.
	* Affiliate/Revenue-share: Yes, Coinbase offers a revenue-sharing program for partners.
2. **Shopify API**
	* Rationale: Shopify is a popular e-commerce platform, and integrating crypto-shield with Shopify will enable businesses to securely accept crypto payments.
	* Free-tier limits: 500 requests per day
	* Integration effort: Large (L)
	* Value-add: Enhances the security and trust of crypto transactions for Shopify merchants.
	* Affiliate/Revenue-share: Yes, Shopify offers an affiliate program with revenue-sharing opportunities.
3. **Stripe API**
	* Rationale: Stripe is a well-established payment processing platform, and integrating crypto-shield with Stripe will expand its crypto payment capabilities.
	* Free-tier limits: 100 requests per day
	* Integration effort: Medium (M)
	* Value-add: Provides businesses with a secure and reliable way to process crypto payments.
	* Affiliate/Revenue-share: Yes, Stripe offers a revenue-sharing program for partners.
4. **MetaMask API**
	* Rationale: MetaMask is a popular cryptocurrency wallet, and integrating crypto-shield with MetaMask will enable secure and seamless crypto transactions.
	* Free-tier limits: 100 requests per day
	* Integration effort: Small (S)
	* Value-add: Enhances the security and usability of crypto transactions for MetaMask users.
	* Affiliate/Revenue-share: No
5. **Binance API**
	* Rationale: Binance is a leading cryptocurrency exchange, and integrating crypto-shield with Binance will provide businesses with access to a wide range of crypto assets.
	* Free-tier limits: 100 requests per day
	* Integration effort: Medium (M)
	* Value-add: Expands the crypto asset options for businesses, enhancing their payment flexibility.
	* Affiliate/Revenue-share: Yes, Binance offers a revenue-sharing program for partners.
6. **QuickBooks API**
	* Rationale: QuickBooks is a popular accounting and bookkeeping platform, and integrating crypto-shield with QuickBooks will enable businesses to securely manage their crypto transactions and accounting.
	* Free-tier limits: 500 requests per day
	* Integration effort: Large (L)
	* Value-add: Streamlines crypto transaction accounting and bookkeeping for businesses.
	* Affiliate/Revenue-share: Yes, QuickBooks offers an affiliate program with revenue-sharing opportunities.
7. **Kraken API**
	* Rationale: Kraken is a reputable cryptocurrency exchange, and integrating crypto-shield with Kraken will provide businesses with access to a secure and reliable crypto payment platform.
	* Free-tier limits: 100 requests per day
	* Integration effort: Medium (M)
	* Value-add: Enhances the security and trust of crypto transactions for businesses.
	* Affiliate/Revenue-share: Yes, Kraken offers a revenue-sharing program for partners.

Prioritization of these partner targets will be based on the potential revenue-sharing opportunities, integration effort, and value-add for crypto-shield users. The initial focus will be on integrating with Coinbase, Shopify, and Stripe, given their large user bases and revenue-sharing programs.
