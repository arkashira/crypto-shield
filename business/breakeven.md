### Key Takeaways

1. **Low variable cost** – $0.17 per active user keeps margins high.  
2. **Premium tier drives fastest break‑even** – only 3 users needed to recover CAC.  
3. **$10 K MRR** is achievable with a modest user base (≈ 100 Premium users) or a larger mix of tiers.  
4. **CAC vs. LTV** – Even at the high end of CAC ($400), LTV remains > $1,700 for Standard and > $5,500 for Premium, ensuring healthy profitability.  

These figures provide a concrete roadmap for scaling crypto‑shield while maintaining financial discipline.
