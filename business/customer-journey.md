# Customer Journey Map
## Overview
The customer journey map for crypto-shield outlines the phases a potential customer goes through from initial awareness to expansion of the product. The following sections break down each phase, including trigger events, friction points, user emotions, opportunities to delight, and key metrics.

## Aware
* **Trigger Event**: Business experiences a crypto payment security breach or hears about the risks associated with crypto transactions.
* **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness about crypto payment security solutions, perceived complexity of implementing a new security tool.
* **User Emotions**: Concern, uncertainty, fear of financial loss.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Educational content (blog posts, webinars) highlighting the importance of crypto payment security, case studies of businesses that have successfully mitigated risks using crypto-shield.
* **Metric**: Website traffic, social media engagement, lead generation (e.g., 500 new leads per quarter).

## Consider
* **Trigger Event**: Business researches crypto payment security solutions, reads reviews, and compares features.
* **Friction Points**: Difficulty in understanding the technical aspects of crypto-shield, concerns about integration with existing systems.
* **User Emotions**: Curiosity, skepticism, hope for a reliable solution.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Free trials or demos, detailed product documentation, customer testimonials, and a clear ROI calculation.
* **Metric**: Free trial sign-ups (e.g., 200 per month), sales-qualified leads (e.g., 50 per quarter).

## Try
* **Trigger Event**: Business decides to trial crypto-shield, sets up an account, and begins testing its features.
* **Friction Points**: Technical difficulties during onboarding, uncertainty about how to use certain features.
* **User Emotions**: Excitement, anticipation, potential frustration if expectations are not met.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Prompt and helpful customer support, intuitive user interface, clear and concise tutorials.
* **Metric**: Trial-to-paid conversion rate (e.g., 30%), customer satisfaction (CSAT) score (e.g., 85%).

## Adopt
* **Trigger Event**: Business decides to adopt crypto-shield as their crypto payment security and custody management tool.
* **Friction Points**: Integration challenges, potential downtime during setup.
* **User Emotions**: Relief, satisfaction, confidence in their security choice.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Personalized onboarding experience, dedicated account management, regular security audits and updates.
* **Metric**: Customer retention rate (e.g., 90% after 6 months), net promoter score (NPS) (e.g., 40).

## Expand
* **Trigger Event**: Business experiences the benefits of crypto-shield and considers expanding its use within the organization or recommending it to partners.
* **Friction Points**: None anticipated, as the business is already satisfied with the product.
* **User Emotions**: Loyalty, advocacy, potential interest in additional features or services.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Regular feedback sessions, priority feature requests, strategic partnership opportunities.
* **Metric**: Customer lifetime value (CLV) increase (e.g., 20% year-over-year), referral rate (e.g., 2 new customers per existing customer per year).

By understanding and addressing the needs and emotions of potential customers at each phase of their journey, crypto-shield can effectively attract, retain, and expand its customer base.
