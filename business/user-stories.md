# User Stories

## Epic 1: Secure Crypto Payment Processing

**User Story 1.1**
- **As a** business owner,
- **I want** to securely process crypto payments,
- **So that** I can accept payments without the risk of fraud or theft.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- The system must support multiple cryptocurrencies.
- The system must integrate with existing payment gateways.
- The system must provide real-time transaction monitoring.
- The system must have a fraud detection mechanism.
- The system must generate detailed transaction reports.

**Complexity:** L

**User Story 1.2**
- **As a** financial analyst,
- **I want** to track and analyze crypto payment trends,
- **So that** I can make informed business decisions.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- The system must provide analytics dashboards.
- The system must support historical data analysis.
- The system must allow for customizable reports.
- The system must integrate with existing analytics tools.
- The system must provide alerts for unusual transaction patterns.

**Complexity:** M

## Epic 2: Custody Management

**User Story 2.1**
- **As a** security officer,
- **I want** to manage crypto custody securely,
- **So that** I can protect the business from internal and external threats.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- The system must support multi-signature wallets.
- The system must provide role-based access control.
- The system must have a secure key management system.
- The system must support regular security audits.
- The system must provide alerts for unauthorized access attempts.

**Complexity:** L

**User Story 2.2**
- **As a** compliance officer,
- **I want** to ensure regulatory compliance for crypto custody,
- **So that** the business can avoid legal penalties.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- The system must support compliance with relevant regulations.
- The system must provide detailed audit logs.
- The system must support regular compliance reporting.
- The system must provide alerts for non-compliance issues.
- The system must support integration with compliance management tools.

**Complexity:** M

## Epic 3: Risk Mitigation

**User Story 3.1**
- **As a** risk manager,
- **I want** to identify and mitigate risks associated with crypto payments,
- **So that** I can protect the business from financial losses.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- The system must provide risk assessment tools.
- The system must support risk mitigation strategies.
- The system must provide real-time risk alerts.
- The system must support historical risk analysis.
- The system must integrate with existing risk management tools.

**Complexity:** L

**User Story 3.2**
- **As a** business owner,
- **I want** to receive alerts for potential risks,
- **So that** I can take proactive measures to mitigate them.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- The system must provide customizable alert thresholds.
- The system must support multiple alert channels (email, SMS, etc.).
- The system must provide detailed alert reports.
- The system must support historical alert analysis.
- The system must integrate with existing alert management tools.

**Complexity:** S

## Epic 4: User Management and Access Control

**User Story 4.1**
- **As a** system administrator,
- **I want** to manage user access and permissions,
- **So that** I can ensure only authorized users can access sensitive data.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- The system must support role-based access control.
- The system must provide detailed user activity logs.
- The system must support regular user access reviews.
- The system must provide alerts for unauthorized access attempts.
- The system must integrate with existing identity management tools.

**Complexity:** M

**User Story 4.2**
- **As a** security officer,
- **I want** to enforce strong authentication mechanisms,
- **So that** I can protect the system from unauthorized access.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- The system must support multi-factor authentication.
- The system must provide secure password management.
- The system must support regular password expiration and complexity requirements.
- The system must provide alerts for failed authentication attempts.
- The system must integrate with existing authentication tools.

**Complexity:** M
